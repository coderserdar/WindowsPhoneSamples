In this scenario, the app will update its badge using periodic updates which will poll a web service to retrieve tile XML content.

As with polling for tile updates, your app can poll your web server for badge updates as well. Once your app calls the polling API, the system will poll the specified URI on a regular basis and update your badge with the returned notification content.

Enter a URL to poll, choose a frequency, and press "Start periodic updates" to begin polling for badge updates
