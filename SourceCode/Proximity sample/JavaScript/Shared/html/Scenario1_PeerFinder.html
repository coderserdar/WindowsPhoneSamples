Click 'Advertise' (on both devices) and then enter proximity. The PeerFinder will link the devices and provide a StreamSocket so they can send messages.

Alternatively, click "Browse for Peers" to find nearby peers and connect to them using "Connect To a Peer" button. The peer application will respond to the incoming connection request and show the "Accept Connection Request" button. After clicking "Accept", click "Send Message" to send a message typed in the text box.
